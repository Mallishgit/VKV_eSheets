Physical Quantity.
  Anything that can be measured and given (assigned) a numeric value is a Physical Quantity.
Eg: Yeshas is 5ft tall . Here we are measuring Yesha's height.

Raghava is very angry. Here anger cannot be measured, so anger is NOT a physical quantity.

Physical Units.
  A number alone cannot give us full information about the measurement . We need a measuring unit.
Eg: Consider this question: "How long do you need to pain?" Answer : 5.
Is it 5 minutes, 5 hours, 5 days? Here minutes , hours and days are called units.
A number along with units give the right meaning for a measurement.
Eg: 5 kg , 2 s (seconds), 7 meter (m)
