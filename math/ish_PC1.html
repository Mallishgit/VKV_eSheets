Does Order matter?
  In some situations order matters and in some situations it doesn't.

Order Matters (Order is important)
When we are spelling a word order matters. meat, is different from tame , is different from team.
What do you think is the difference between the three words highlighted above?
Yes, it is the order in which the letters appear that matters.

Order doesn't matter (Order is NOT important)
Now let us say m=mouse, e=eagle, a=ant , t=tiger and you choose the animals in this order 'meat' or "team" or "mate" or any other order - you would have selected the same four animals. Here order does NOT matter.

Permutation : Arrangements where Order matters is called Permutation .
Combination : Arrangements where Order does NOT matter is called Combination
