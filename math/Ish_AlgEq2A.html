Example
 
  1) Anita has 4 more apples than Reena. A=R+4
 If Anita has 31 apples, then how many does Rena have?
Equations   31=R+4 =>  31- 4=R+4 - 4   =>  27=R

  2) Reena has 4 more books than Suma. R=S+4 
If Suma has 31 books, how many does Reena have?   R=31+4   =>  R=35
