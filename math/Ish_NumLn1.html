Number Line-1
 
Number Line. is an useful tool to make sense of arithmetic operations.

Here the numbers to the right of 0 are positive (+ sign is optional) .Numbers to the left of 0 are negative ( - is a must).
Positive numbers are used to indicate profit, gain ,addition.
Negative numbers are used to indicate loss, reduction, loan, borrowing , subtraction.
