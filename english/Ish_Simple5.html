Simple form - 5
 

Question words, like Who, What  , when indicating a 3rd person, singular may require the usage of simple form of the verbs.
Eg: Who takes care of the house? Here 'Who" is referring to a singular so the simple form 'takes' is used.

Simple form of the verb requires us to consider the number (singular/plural) when used with question words 'Where' , 'When' , 'How' .
Eg: When do we reach Bengaluru? (do, reach) . ['do' is used due to 'we' (plural)]
When does he reach Bengaluru?  (do, reach). ['does' is used due to he (singular)]
